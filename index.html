      Well no, but if you move your company offshore you can probably ignore it.

      Absolutely, we are happy to take your money in all forms.

      We only hire our customers, so subscribe for a minimum of 6 months and then let’s talk.

    • What was that story about losing my data?

      That was a total misunderstanding. It was only a small subset of data and it was mostly just old receipts.

      You just tell us what data you need a report for, and we get our kids to create beautiful charts for you using only the finest crayons.

      In life it’s really better to never expect anything at all.

      Send us an email and we will send you a copy of your password in plain text.

    • Can I export my data?

      Yes, you can export your data as a PDF file that is almost impossible to parse.

    • My bank feed isn’t working, what do I do?

      This is a known issue. We recommend you just manually enter your transactions for now.
